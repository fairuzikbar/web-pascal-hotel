About Us

Hoteliers since 1897, Pascal has travelled the world in search of the most fascinating destinations without ever forgetting the finest traditions of its European heritage. With heritage hotels, luxury holiday resorts, business and spa hotels around the world, every Pascal hotel is unique, designed for guests who value five-star holidays and luxury getaways.

In an industry dominated by consolidation, Pascal is proud to be Europe's oldest luxury hotel group. Our carefully curated collection comprises historic landmark properties, iconic city hotels, outstanding resorts, and prestigious residences. Each one is imbued with the quality guests have come to expect from Pascal while embracing the cultural traditions of its location.
